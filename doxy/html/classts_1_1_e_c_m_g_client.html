Detailed Description

A DVB-ECMG client which acts as a DVB-SCS.

Restriction: The target ECMG shall support only current/next control words in ECM, meaning CW_per_msg = 2 and lead_CW = 1.

See also
DVB standard ETSI TS 103.197 V1.4.1 for ECMG <=> SCS protocol.
