Detailed Description

Representation of an AVC_video_descriptor.

This MPG-defined descriptor is not defined in ISO/IEC 13818-1, ITU-T Rec. H.222.0. See its "Amendment 3: Transport of AVC video over ITU-T Rec. H.222.0 | ISO/IEC 13818-1 streams" (document W5771), section 2.6.54.
