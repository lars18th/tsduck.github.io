Detailed Description

Representation of a date in DVB SimulCrypt protocols (ETSI TS 103 197).

A DVB SimulCrypt date is represented on 8 bytes:

  • year: 2 bytes
  • month: 1 byte
  • day: 1 byte
  • hour: 1 byte
  • minute: 1 byte
  • second: 1 byte
  • hundredth: 1 byte
