◆ EntropyMode

enum ts::Scrambling::EntropyMode

Control word entropy reduction.

This is a way to reduce the 'entropy' of control words to 48 bits, according to DVB regulations.

Enumerator
FULL_CW 

Keep the full 64-bit control word.

REDUCE_ENTROPY 

Reduce the entropy of the control word to 48 bits.
